Goal

Strengthen waist and improve core stability

Injury Consideration

Waist

Body Type

Hourglass

Sport Preference

Class

Exercise Time

20 minutes

Warm-up (5 minutes)

  • Light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, jogging in place)
  • Dynamic stretching (e.g., leg swings, arm circles)

Core Exercises (10 minutes)

  • Plank: Hold for 30 seconds, rest for 30 seconds. Repeat 5 times.
  • Side Plank: Hold for 30 seconds on each side, rest for 30 seconds. Repeat 5 times.
  • Russian Twists: 20 repetitions (10 on each side).
  • Bicycle Crunches: 20 repetitions.
  • Bird Dog: 10 repetitions on each side.

Lower Body Exercises (5 minutes)

  • Squats: 20 repetitions (focus on keeping core engaged).
  • Lunges: 10 repetitions on each leg.
  • Curtsy Lunges: 10 repetitions on each leg.
  • Hip Thrusts: 20 repetitions.

Cool-down (5 minutes)

  • Static stretching (e.g., hamstring stretch, quad stretch)
  • Foam rolling (optional)

Tips

  • Listen to your body and rest when needed.
  • Focus on quality over quantity.
  • Gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts.
  • Avoid exercises that put excessive strain on your waist.
  • Consider consulting a physical therapist or trainer for guidance.
